Yoon Wah Snow Beer 

The first and the only one Yoon Wah Snow Beer at Ipoh, Yoon Wah Snow Beer gives you the feeling of beyond the frosty. We keep the beer and the wineglass together in the refrigerator and then infuse the wineglass with beer to create an ice blender beverage with the flavor of the beer.
