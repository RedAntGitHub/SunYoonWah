The Origin

The predecessor of Cafe Sun Yoon was a grocery store that had been in business for decades. It was known that several tables were placed outside the grocery store in 1999 to sell beer. Afterward, it officially transformed in 2002 and began to sell snow beer and food.

About Us

From the beginning of a single storefront, started with two tables, the business has expanded over the years, so far we already have 3 business names, six stores, one air-conditioned hall, two open-air environments, and 200 tables. Other than the late night business hours, we are seeking further opportunity to begin our business on the morning hours.
